Who Are We?

This is April is one of the leading fashion brands in Indonesia. We focus in fulfilling woman’s desire for fashionable, affordable, and high-quality clothing and accessories. We started in 2012 with only few people on board. Basically, everyone was doing everything they could to build this business. Now, our company houses 300+ people, and serve 60+ stores all around Indonesia. Our team members are a diverse bunch of people who love to think big and make it happen.

In This is April, we value and support our people to be their best versions of themselves. We learn and inspire each other in every way possible. When you join our team, be ready be a part of something big!

Above all else, when you start here, you can go anywhere!
